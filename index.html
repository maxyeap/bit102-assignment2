About Seven Seas

Seven Seas Café & Bar is a seaside cafe that serves up fresh and tasty western seafood dishes, as well as delicious cocktails. Our mission is to provide our guests with an unforgettable dining experience, while showcasing the best of Langkawi's natural beauty. With our prime location on Cenang Beach, Seven Seas Café & Bar is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy some of the best seafood in town. Whether you're looking for a light snack or something more substantial, Seven Seas Café & Bar has something to tantalize your taste buds. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, and our talented chefs strive to create innovative and exciting new dishes for our guests to enjoy.
